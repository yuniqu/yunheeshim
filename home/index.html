Research

I study how people interpret and enact governance on platforms—how design choices, policy framing, and enforcement signals affect perceived fairness, accountability, and user behavior.

Creator-led moderation & justice
How creators choose strategies (reply/delete/ignore) under attention & governance constraints.
  • Justice frameworks (retributive vs restorative)
  • Impression management and perceived safety
  • Design implications for platform governance
Procedural fairness in flagging
How review transparency, voice, and consistency shape trust and intervention behavior.
  • Survey experiments & interface manipulations
  • Perceived legitimacy of enforcement
  • Human vs AI moderation cues
